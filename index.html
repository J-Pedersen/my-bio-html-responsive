My Hobbies

Cooking

I love to cook. I regularly find myself just cooking stuff to freeze or to give away because I felt like making it. When I do cook you can be assured that it will not be health food because I have not mastered the ability to make food taste good without adding a pound of butter and tons of salt.

The way I see it we only live once and if I am eating food I am not enjoying just to stay healthier and live longer what is the point in existing longer if you are depriving yourself of some of the things which make life worth living.

WoodWorking

Some people read, others play games and the list goes on. Woodworking in my workshop is where I go to relax and get away from the stress which build up daily and doesnt seem to have an outlet. It would be nice if I had the time to do wood working more regularly but I am extremely busy and I basically am doing other things from like 4am until midnight 7 days a week.

When I have on rare occations have time to spend in my shop I have made some pretty nice pieces of furniture. People have told me "you should make those things to sell them you would make a lot of money" but what they don't understand is that I don't build things for money. I build things because i had an idea and I wanted to create it. Also if i did build things for money I would have to chage 10 times what they are worth to make it profitable because I will sometimes work on a piece of furniture off and on for over a year before I call it good. Its never really done because I know where all the flaws are and they will always bother me so at some point I just have to stop myself and say this is done.

Eventually he might understand that I will never let him win.

I choose to get my Associate's Degree in Web Devlopment. It was 100% paid for without any money coming out of my pocket so it was really an oppertunity which I would have been a fool to pass up the offer.

It took me about a year to finish my Associates Degree and I was working fulltime while going to school. After I graduated I never really put the efforts needed into doing anything with that degree or tried to continue my education because I had a fulltime job which I was very skilled at and I was already making a very good living doing it.

Now that I am getting older I find myself regreting that decision to not push my education further because I am getting too old to be crawling on the floor under equipment to repair it. Which is why I am now going back to college to have the ability to still work but from my desk chair.
